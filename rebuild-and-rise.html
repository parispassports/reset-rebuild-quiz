Welcome to Rebuild & Rise.

I want to start by saying how excited I am to have you here. This program was created for the woman who is ready to move beyond just starting over. You've already begun the process of rebuilding — and now, this is where we rise. With more structure. More strength. More intention. And most importantly, more self-trust.

Inside this experience, you are not being asked to force, punish, or override your body. You are being invited to build in a way that is more supportive, more sustainable, and far more powerful over time.

So as you move through this program, let this be your reminder: you do not need to chase random workouts or all-or-nothing intensity to make progress. We build through repetition, structure, and the kind of consistency your body can actually trust.

Give yourself permission to move with intention. To learn your patterns. To honor the process. And to recognize that strength is not only something you perform — it is something you build.

I'm so glad you're here. Let's get started.

Nutrition
Structured Eating
Without Extremes
Carb needs change day to day. Higher activity days require more fuel. Rest days feel better with fewer carbs. Listen to your body and adjust as needed.
The Protein Anchor Rule
Every meal contains a protein source. It is your non-negotiable.
Eggs • Greek yogurt • Chicken • Turkey • Beef • Fish • Tofu / Tempeh • Protein shake
Protein stabilizes blood sugar, supports recovery, and improves satiety.
Intensity-Based Carb Regulation
We do not eliminate carbohydrates. We regulate them.
🟢
High-Intensity / Lower Body Days
Include a carb source pre- or post-training → rice, potatoes, fruit, oats
🟡
Moderate Training Days
Balanced plate → protein + carb + fat + vegetables
🔴
Restore / Low-Intensity Days
Protein + vegetables + moderate carbs → keep meals steady, not excessive
Your Three Carb Days
🔴
Low Carb Day
1–2 servings • 70–120g complex carbohydrates
Breakfast
Egg scramble with spinach & peppers (avocado slices)
Snack
Pinwheel turkey & provolone with lettuce
Lunch
Large mixed greens salad w/ grilled chicken, salmon, or shrimp (balsamic dressing)
Snack
Greek yogurt & mixed berries
Dinner
Grilled or blackened protein (fish, turkey, chicken, lean beef) + non-starchy veggies (broccoli, zucchini)
Snack
Whey shake (low carb)
🟡
Moderate Carb Day
~130–200g carbohydrates • 0.8–1.0g protein per lb bodyweight
Breakfast
Eggs with whole grain toast + apple or banana
Snack
Protein bar
Lunch
Brown rice or quinoa bowl, grilled chicken/fish/turkey, roasted vegetables
Dinner
Lean protein (lean beef, fish, chicken) + rice or sweet potato & steamed veggies
Snack
Protein low carb shake
🟢
High Carb Day
220–300g carbohydrates
Breakfast
Oatmeal with nuts + berries & 2 sausage links or bacon
Snack
Pear and berry crumble
Lunch
Egg boil or onion boil
Snack
Protein smoothie
Dinner
Spaghetti or leftovers
